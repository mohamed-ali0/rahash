## ğŸ“˜ ARCHITECTURAL DECISIONS

### Backend Philosophy
- **Blueprint Pattern:** Each feature area has its own blueprint
- **Separation of Concerns:** Models, routes, and utils are independent
- **Token Authentication:** Centralized in auth utility

### Frontend Philosophy

- **Utility-First:** Common functions in utility modules
- **Progressive Enhancement:** Works with or without JS modules
